03 • Arrival / Dolazak

Check-in instructions

Upute za prijavu

The website should explain the general process, while the key-safe code should be sent directly to the guest on the date of arrival.

Stranica treba objašnjavati opći postupak, dok se kod za sef s ključem treba slati gostu izravno na dan dolaska.

1

Find Entrance 7

Pronađite Ulaz 7

Go to Lamela / Entrance 7 on the south side of the Orca building. Near the mailboxes you will see the mini key safes labeled “MoRent”.

Dođite do Lamele / Ulaza 7 na južnoj strani zgrade Orca. Kod poštanskih sandučića vidjet ćete mini sefove za ključeve s oznakom „MoRent“.

2

Open the correct safe

Otvorite odgovarajući sef

The key for the reserved apartment will be in the safe marked for that apartment. The access code is sent directly to the guest on the arrival date.

Ključ rezerviranog apartmana nalazi se u sefu označenom za taj apartman. Pristupni kod gost dobiva izravno na dan dolaska.

3

Use the blue chip

Upotrijebite plavi čip

A blue access chip is attached to the apartment key. Tap it on the reader near the intercom to open the main entrance.

Na ključu apartmana nalazi se plavi pristupni čip. Prislonite ga na čitač pored interfona kako biste otvorili glavni ulaz.

4

Times

Vremena

Check-in from 14:00. Check-out by 11:00. After departure, return the key to the same mini safe and scramble the safe numbers for security.

Prijava od 14:00. Odjava do 11:00. Nakon odlaska vratite ključ u isti mini sef i izmiješajte brojeve na sefu radi sigurnosti.

04 • Parking

Parking information

Informacije o parkingu

  • About 50 free parking spaces are available on the north and east sides of the building.
  • City parking is available on Knez Višeslava Street and near the sports hall.
  • A private paid parking area is on the west side of the building.
  • South of the building, near the sports hall, there is parking charged once upon entry.
  • Mepas Mall is about 50 meters away and can also be used as a practical landmark and parking option.
  • Na sjevernoj i istočnoj strani zgrade dostupno je oko 50 besplatnih parking mjesta.
  • Gradski parking dostupan je u Ulici kneza Višeslava i u blizini gradske dvorane.
  • Na zapadnoj strani zgrade nalazi se privatni parking koji se plaća.
  • Južno od zgrade, kod gradske dvorane, nalazi se parking koji se naplaćuje po ulazu.
  • Mepas Mall udaljen je oko 50 metara i može poslužiti kao praktičan orijentir i opcija za parking.
